⚠ IV CRUSH WARNING
Implied Volatility spikes before earnings, then collapses immediately after — "IV crush." A stock can rise 5% and your call still LOSES money because IV collapse offsets the gain. Use spreads to dampen IV crush. Or buy straddles EARLY in week (lower IV), not day-of.
